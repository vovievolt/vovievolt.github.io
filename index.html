My portfolio of work

Fully recyclable 3d printed phone holder

Designed to function as a desktop sitting phone cradle, I modelled this using 123d Design to suit the stakeholders needs practically and to be visually pleasing. It has multiple compartments varying in size to be used as storage for various stationary items such as clips, pens, pins etc. Part of the project was doing research into what 3d printable materials would be the most environmentally sustainable. Being made out of Polyvinyl chloride means that it is not too heavy, but still fully printable and fully recyclable.
